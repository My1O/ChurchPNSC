Sacred Heart of Jesus has been in existence since 1925. It is located between Willow Glen and downtown San Jose serves the community in Northern San Jose. Most of its parishioners are of Latin descent and Spanish is their main language. The Parish is next to Martha’s Kitchen that serves the poor and hungry in that area and the urban Catholic Sacred Heart Nativity School.
